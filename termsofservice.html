Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

Last updated: 2024-01-27

1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) governs the use of the services provided by Harmony Hub. By accessing and using these services, users agree to comply with this AUP and ensure that their use of the services is legal and ethical.

2. Acceptable Use

The services must be used for lawful purposes only and with respect for others' rights and property. Examples of acceptable use include:

  • Accessing and communicating with the service for personal or internal business purposes.
  • Sharing content or data that is not illegal, harmful, or offensive.
  • Protecting the confidentiality and integrity of personal and company data.

3. Unacceptable Use

Examples of unacceptable use include, but are not limited to:

  • Violating any laws or regulations.
  • Harassing, threatening, or offending others.
  • Distributing viruses, malware, or other harmful software.
  • Attempting unauthorized access to systems or data.

4. System Security

Users are responsible for the security of their accounts and must not share passwords or other access credentials.

5. Monitoring and Enforcement

Harmony Hub reserves the right to monitor the use of its services to ensure compliance with this AUP. Violations may result in suspension or termination of access to services.

6. Reporting Violations

Users should report any violations of the AUP to nathaniel.mais@dcmail.ca or jaden.parris@dcmail.ca.

7. Modifications to the AUP

Harmony hub may modify this AUP at any time and will publish the updated policy on its website.

By using Harmony Hub's services, you agree to abide by the most recent version of this AUP.
